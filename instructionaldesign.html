Digital Adoption Case Studies

These case studies highlight measurable results from my work, including reducing errors, increasing adoption, and accelerating delivery.

Prevented a $1M Billing Error

Problem: A coordination team uncovered that a $1M rebill occurred when a user resubmitted an outdated rate sheet. Salesforce had no guardrails to prevent this.

Solution: Built an invisible launcher to detect outdated dates and configured a high-visibility blocking warning.

Outcome: Stopped high-risk billing errors before they reached Finance.

Reduced IT Backlog & Quick Insights

Problem: Recruiters needed a quick way to assess candidate engagement in Salesforce. A Dev backlog item requested a color-coded indicator.

Solution: Partnered with the Product Owner to remove the item from Dev backlog. Designed WalkMe launchers that display color-coded levels dynamically.

Outcome: Delivered a production-ready solution in under 36 hours.

Emergency UI Removal in Salesforce

Problem: Salesforce legacy “Send List Email” option was creating unnecessary work and workflow errors.

Solution: Applied WalkMe custom CSS to hide the option for Account Managers.

Outcome: Achieved a fast fix without requiring Dev resources.
