Bio

Michelle Hulan is a poet and writer. Her work has appeared in the Toronto Journal, HAD, the Citron Review, RHINO, and elsewhere. She received her MA in English from the University of Ottawa and now lives in Brooklyn with her family, where she can be found knitting.
